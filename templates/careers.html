Autonomy

You can work whenever you want and however you want. At cfautocare we focus on the destination, and let you focus on the passage because we know that everyone has their own way of getting things done.

Merit

If you're going to do something, do it right. And if you don't know how to do it right, keep learning and practicing until you do. We strive for excellence and as long as you have the desire, all it takes is time and effort.

Growth

You decide where to stop and you decide where to start. We have many positions available from physical labor, to human relations, to strategy, to engineering, and etc... And we make sure to remove obstacles that hinder our team's growth to foster great products and services.
